About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Center for Humans and Machines (CHM) at Max Planck Institute for Human Development. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from The University of Colorado Boulder in 2020, advised by Dr. Qin Lv and Dr. Chenhao Tan.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computational social science and machine learning, with an emphasize on bridging the gap between online and offline worlds for advancing the understanding of human behavior and society.

I am also generally interested in data science, natural language processing, human + AI, and recommender systems.
